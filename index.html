Hi I’m Jody, a web developer based in London. Currently making ebooks for Penguin and learning 
Javascript.

I’ve always had a passion for technology. I used to rip apart old hard drives to see how they worked and built my first computer at 14. I remember, when we first got connected to the internet, asking Jeeves, very politely, if he could please tell me what time is was in New York as we had no idea how search engines worked.

Since then technology, and especially the internet, has come a long way and there are very few places that are not touched by it to some degree. I am always awed by the impact that one person, a computer, and an internet connection can have on peoples lives.

My career to date has always gravitated towards using tech in some way and in my current job I use HTML & CSS daily to produce ebooks. I enjoy the role but have always found the platform severely lagging behind the open web and have always dreamt of making the leap into web development and doing something really impactful.

This is where Founders & Coders comes in. So far, i've struggled to fit learning everything I need around working full time and when a colleague got into Founders & Coders in the Autumn and told me about it, I knew it was a perfect fit for me.
